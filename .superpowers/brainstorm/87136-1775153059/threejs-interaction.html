How does the 3D scene react to a search?

The moment you submit a mood query, the Three.js background transforms. Pick which behavior feels most right.

Wormhole Dive

On submit, camera flies through a pulsing wormhole tunnel. Stars stream past. Results materialize as glowing orbs when you come out the other side.

Big Bang Explosion

Submit triggers a supernova burst — thousands of particles explode outward from center. Each song result is a glowing planet that settles into orbit around you.

Mood Morph

The central blob continuously morphs shape and color based on your query. Happy = warm, fast. Sad = slow, cool. Glitch effects fire on submit. Results float as satellites.

This is the signature animation — the money shot of the UI
